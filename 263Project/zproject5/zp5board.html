Department

Department of Event Management

Event Planning: We encourage creativity and fresh ideas for activities to ensure the student community's activities are vibrant and interesting. From cultural festivals to summits, social events to celebrity meet-and-greets, you will be involved in planning and organizing various events to resonate widely. Event Execution: Once planning is complete, you will be responsible for seamless execution and progression of events, ensuring every detail meets expectations and brings enjoyment to the student community. Event Tracking: Maintain close communication with all departments, follow up on event progress, and optimize the schedule to ensure everything proceeds smoothly. Personnel Requirements： We welcome students from diverse backgrounds, as diversity is our strength. However, we also require: Teamwork: Collaborate within a diverse team to achieve great goals. Here, every voice is valued, and every idea has the chance to become reality. Communication Skills: Excellent communication skills are key to collaboration. As a member of the Activities Department, you will need to interact with people from different groups to ensure tasks proceed smoothly and events are flawless. Leadership: Ability to lead team members, coordinate task distribution and workflow, and lead us forward together. Problem-Solving Skills: Facing challenges, we expect you to propose innovative solutions and continuously improve the planning and execution processes of events, taking each event to a higher level.

Department of Technology

The Technical Department is responsible for managing and supporting tasks related to technology and IT. Provides internal technical support, solves technical problems; develops technical strategies to assist the alliance's growth needs; offers training and development plans to ensure members have the necessary technical skills and knowledge. Recruits, manages, and leads the technical team to ensure high performance and collaboration. Specific tasks include but are not limited to building various websites; debugging and maintaining technical equipment; filming and post-editing for events.

Department of Media

The Media Department, serving as the main force for external publicity and internal information dissemination, cooperates actively with all departments to accurately and timely convey the latest news and exciting activities of the association to every student and cooperating organization. Here, interesting and fulfilling work is filled with new challenges and opportunities! In the Media Department, we provide a creative and energetic platform where you can learn cutting-edge media knowledge and skills! We are not only messengers of information but also guardians of dreams!

Job Responsibilities：

  • Use tools like ShowMe and Canva for layout design of WeChat posts.
  • Design and produce posters, invitations, logos, and other promotional materials.
  • Write and edit press releases, event reports, etc.
  • Update and maintain social media platforms like Little Red Book, TikTok, Instagram, etc.
  • Photography and video recording at event sites.
Personnel Requirements：
  • Good teamwork and communication skills.
  • High execution ability and responsibility.
  • Strong interest or experience in learning media skills (e.g., photography, editing, posting, poster design).
  • Prior experience in operating social media and WeChat public accounts is preferred.

Department of Public Relationship

Internal Relations: Coordinate with the headquarters' external relations department, while maintaining close contact with other departments of the Eastern Association to ensure information sharing transparency. Additionally, responsible for collecting feedback and suggestions related to internal cooperation to continuously improve collaboration processes and plan internal events to bring entertainment and vitality to the team.
External Relations: Establish and cooperate with partnering student unions and sponsors, confirm long-term partnership relationships, and promote cooperation among student unions. In the process of maintaining cooperation relationships, actively organize entertainment activities to enhance the team cohesion of the External Relations Department, reflecting an entertaining work atmosphere. Also responsible for finding new potential sponsors and cooperation partners to expand the cooperation network of the student union.

Department of Human Resources

The mission of the Human Resources Department is to recruit, train, and manage outstanding talents for the alliance. We continuously infuse fresh blood into the alliance through carefully designed recruitment processes. While assisting members to smoothly integrate into the alliance, we also uncover students' deeper interests and potential, becoming the one to spot the "steed" among the horses.

Core work of the Human Resources Department includes:

Talent Recruitment:

  • We will regularly receive resumes and basic information from applicants, and based on the needs and core values of the alliance, filter the suitable candidates and arrange interviews.
  • Members of the Human Resources Department will be fully involved in the interview process, helping departments find members that are suitable for them.
Organization Management:
  • Design the overall framework of the alliance, and establish ways of cooperation between departments. Help alliance members with integration and capacity building, focusing on the sustainable development of the alliance.
Talent Operation:
  • Help alliance members develop "inward" qualities, including improvement in capabilities, cognition, and psychological aspects.
  • Help alliance members enhance "outward" qualities, including networking, teamwork, and building social influence.

Finance Department

Responsible for cooperating with relevant departments or teams to establish the theme of each event; coordinate the resources needed for activities, such as venues, personnel, equipment, etc., to ensure the smooth progress of activities. Write timely and attractive posts based on the latest news, results, or related news of the association. Through communication and coordination with other departments or organizations, develop and oversee the financial budget, plan, and objectives of the association.

Secretariat Department

Assist the chairman, vice-chairman, and department leaders in handling related matters, send reports to department leaders promptly after notifications from the chairman and vice-chairman, and be responsible for contacting relevant departments to complete tasks. Clearly understand the needs of each department, sponsors, and any related organization during events, and summarize to report to the chairman and vice-chairman. Update meeting records and pre-event research records timely.

Department of Event Planning

Invite guests from different fields to produce radio programs: every two weeks, the radio program will invite different people to share views and stories. Provide more ideas and thoughts for other departments. Expand new projects and try more possibilities.
